# Research

My research focuses on understanding and engineering protein–protein interactions, especially in therapeutic contexts where mutations can rewire binding interfaces.

---

## Themes

### 1) Mutation-driven interface rewiring
I look at how sequence changes reshape binding through altered electrostatics, sterics, and hydrophobic packing—often revealed only through dynamics.

### 2) Dynamics-informed interpretability
I develop/benchmark analysis methods that convert long MD trajectories into **interpretable interaction signatures**.

### 3) Scalable structure prediction pipelines
I build pipelines for AlphaFold/related tools to support **large-scale screening**, fast failure detection, and principled ranking.

---

## Methods & Tools

- **MD:** NAMD / OpenMM / Amber, trajectory analysis, free-energy landscapes
- **Structure prediction:** AlphaFold(-Multimer/-3), ColabFold, large-scale screening workflows
- **Analysis:** PCA/UMAP/TICA, interface scoring, covariance-based features
- **Compute:** Slurm, containers, reproducible pipelines

---

## Current Interests

- Better metrics for complex confidence beyond a single score
- Linking “fast convergence” to true complex quality
- Integrating dynamics signals into screening/ranking
